μRPG

About

μRPG (pronounced micro-RPG) is just that, it starts small and and quick to learn yet its inherently modular design gives it infinite expandability. The design philosophy of μRPG is to strip things back to the very basics, and then allow players to add new mechanics as needed through supplemental materials.

This is primarily achieved through the perk pack system, where a character's progression (and access to interesting mechanics) is not tied to a class or race. Instead, characters advance through a series of perk trees, the trunks of which are always accessible to anyone. This system enables the creation of incredibly diverse characters and fosters emergent behaviors and synergies that can be discovered mid-campaign.

This, combined with the skill fusion system, which allows new skills to be created by combining two or more existing ones, it’s almost certain that no two character builds will ever be the same.

All in all this means the game can learned in less than 10 minutes whilst still facilitating a depth of mechanics that allows for it to be used in campaigns spanning years!
